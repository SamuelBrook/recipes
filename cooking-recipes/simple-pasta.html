Simple Pasta Recipe

Simple spaghetti with sauce dish

Let's begin making our simple spaghetti dish. This recipe only takes around 15 minutes to prepare and is super delicious! This recipe is great for people looking for a pasta dish that is easy to prepare, economical and flavourful

What are we waiting for? Let's get started!

Ingredients

  • 250g of spaghetti
  • Half a jar of tomato sauce
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 onion
  • A sprinkling of basil
  • A splash of olive oil (enough to thinly cover the bottom of the frying pan)

Steps

  1. Cut up the onion and the garlic into thin slices (extra thin for the garlic).
  2. Splash olive oil in a frying pan so that it thinly covers the bottom of the pan and set the stove to a medium heat.
  3. Add the onions to the pan and after several minutes the garlic too.
  4. While the onions are cooking, add water to another pan and bring it to the boil. Add the pasta to the water.
  5. Once the onions have a golden brown colour, add the tomato sauce.
  6. When the spaghetti is ready, drain the pasta, add the sauce to the pan and stir thoroughly.
  7. Finally, serve the dish and top it off with the basil as a garnish.
