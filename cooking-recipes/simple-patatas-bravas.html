Simple Patatas Bravas Recipe

Simple traditional Spanish Patatas Bravas Dish

This simple but flavourful traditional dish is a favourite among many Spaniards. Cooked with simple ingredients and with minimal prep time, this dish is perfect for those on a budget or with limited time to spare.

There's no time to waste so let's get cracking!

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 4 large potatos
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 teaspoons of smoked paprika
  • Half a teaspoon of black pepper
  • Quarter of teaspoon of salt
  • A sprinkle of parsley
  • Olive oil
  • Vegetable stock

Steps

  1. Prepare the potatos by chopping them into small cubes.
  • Add olive oil into a frying pan to a depth of half of one of the small potato cubes and heat up the oil on a medium heat.
  • Add the potato cubes to the oil, cook until they have a golden colour, and drain the oil (while cooking start the next step).
  • Cut onions and garlic into very fine pieces.
  • Add a thin layer of oil into another pan, add the onion and then after around three minutes add the garlic.
  • Prepare 300ml of vegetable stock according to the instructions on the packet
  • Once the onions are golden brown, add prepared vegetable stock and mix thoroughly.
  • At this point you can add the smoked paprika, black pepper and salt to the sauce. Again, stir thoroughly.
  • Keep heating the sauce until it is less watery. If you prefer, at this point you can add some flour to thicken the sauce.
  • Once the sauce is at the desired thickness, serve the potatos and pour the sauce carefully on top (not all the sauce has to be added at once).
  • Garnish with the parsely.
