Simple Patatas Bravas Recipe

Simple Traditional Spanish Patatas Bravas Dish

This simple but flavourful traditional dish is a favourite among many Spaniards. Cooked with simple ingredients and with minimal prep time, this dish is perfect for those on a budget or with limited time to spare.

There's no time to waste so let's get cracking!

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 4 large potatos
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 teaspoons of smoked paprika
  • Half a teaspoon of black pepper
  • Quarter of teaspoon of salt
  • A sprinkle of parsley
  • Olive oil
  • Vegetable stock

Steps

  1. Prepare the potatos by chopping them into small cubes.
  2. Add olive oil into a frying pan to a depth of half of one of the small potato cubes and heat up the oil on a medium heat.
  3. Add the potato cubes to the oil, cook until they have a golden colour, and drain the oil (while cooking start the next step).
  4. Cut onions and garlic into very fine pieces.
  5. Add a thin layer of oil into another pan, add the onion and then after around three minutes add the garlic.
  6. Prepare 300ml of vegetable stock according to the instructions on the packet
  7. Once the onions are golden brown, add prepared vegetable stock and mix thoroughly.
  8. At this point you can add the smoked paprika, black pepper and salt to the sauce. Again, stir thoroughly.
  9. Keep heating the sauce until it is less watery. If you prefer, at this point you can add some flour to thicken the sauce.
  10. Once the sauce is at the desired thickness, serve the potatos and pour the sauce carefully on top (not all the sauce has to be added at once).
  11. Garnish with the parsely.
